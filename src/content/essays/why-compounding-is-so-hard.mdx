Compounding is less about formulas and more about emotional endurance.

Small decisions, repeated for years, create outcomes that look sudden in hindsight.

> The challenge is not understanding compounding. The challenge is surviving boredom.

The mind craves visible progress. Compounding often hides progress until late, which is why many people quit just before the curve steepens.
