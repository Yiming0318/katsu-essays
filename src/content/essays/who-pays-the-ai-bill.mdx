The AI story is often told as a model story. In practice, it is a cost-allocation story.

Compute, data pipelines, evaluation, and human oversight all require someone to absorb ongoing expense.

> Every AI product is also a financing model disguised as software.

When we ask who wins in AI, we should first ask who can carry infrastructure costs long enough to find durable demand.
