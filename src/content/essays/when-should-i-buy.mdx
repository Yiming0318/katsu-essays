Most bad entries are not caused by bad ideas. They come from rushed emotional state transitions.

> I don't need a perfect bottom. I need a process I can repeat under stress.

Before buying, I ask three questions: what is my edge, what invalidates it, and how much pain I can absorb before I lose clarity.

![Desk with charts and notes](/katsu-essays/images/essay-inline-chart.svg)

When those answers are written down, execution gets calmer. When they are vague, every candle looks like a signal.
